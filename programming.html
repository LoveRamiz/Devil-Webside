Programming




A programmer sat at his desk, studying different timer apps to understand how they worked. He planned the features: countdown, interactive buttons, and alerts. Step by step, he coded the first BoomTimer using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. He tested it at home, making sure the countdown was accurate and the timer worked smoothly. After fixing small bugs and adjusting the design, the first BoomTimer was ready to use.


After completing the first version, the programmer added a display screen for better visuals. A Christmas hat was placed on top to give a fun UI touch. The countdown and buttons were updated to match the new design. Currently, the app is launch-ready, but debugging and final testing are ongoing. Improvements are being made to ensure smooth countdown and perfect interaction.


The programmer finalized the complete BoomTimer UI, bringing all features together. The countdown works smoothly, with accurate timing and clear display. Sound effects and visuals were added to make the experience interactive. The design now feels polished, stable, and fully ready for use.
